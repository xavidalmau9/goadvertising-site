5 Common Mistakes on Lifeline Applications

March 22, 2026 · GO Advertising — authorized Assurance Wireless partner

Nothing is more frustrating than needing essential phone service and seeing a "Denied" or "Pending" status on your application. Most rejections aren't because you don't qualify — they're because of simple paperwork errors.

Avoid these five common pitfalls:

  1. Address mismatches: the address on your application must match your ID or proof of benefits exactly.
  2. Blurry document photos: when uploading a SNAP or Medicaid card, make sure all four corners are visible and the text is readable.
  3. Duplicate applications: there's only one Lifeline benefit per household — make sure no one else at your address has an active account.
  4. Incorrect Social Security numbers: a single wrong digit triggers an automatic rejection from the National Verifier.
  5. Expired proof: your Medicaid or SNAP documents must be current for 2026.

Get it right the first time — start a fresh, clean application today.
