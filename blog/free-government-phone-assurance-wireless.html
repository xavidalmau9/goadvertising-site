How to Get a Free Government Phone Through Assurance Wireless (2026 Guide)

Updated June 2026 · GO Advertising — authorized Assurance Wireless partner

Yes — if you qualify for the federal Lifeline program, you can get a free smartphone and free monthly service through Assurance Wireless at no cost. GO Advertising (BGOA) is an authorized Assurance Wireless partner that helps eligible applicants enroll. Here's exactly how it works and how to apply.

What you get

Assurance Wireless is a federal Lifeline-supported program. Qualifying customers receive:

  • A free smartphone
  • Free monthly high-speed data
  • Unlimited texts and monthly minutes

There's no cost for the phone or the monthly plan — it's funded by the government Lifeline benefit.

Who qualifies for a free government phone?

You're eligible if you take part in any one of these programs:

  • Medicaid
  • SNAP (food stamps)
  • Supplemental Security Income (SSI)
  • Federal Public Housing Assistance
  • Veterans Pension & Survivors Benefit

You also qualify if your household income is at or below 135% of the Federal Poverty Guidelines. The Lifeline benefit is limited to one per household.

How to apply (step by step)

  1. Confirm you qualify — check the program or income list above.
  2. Gather proof — your Medicaid/SNAP card, a benefit letter, or income documentation.
  3. Apply on the official partner page — assurancewireless.com/partner/BGOA.
  4. Submit your details and proof of eligibility.
  5. Get your phone — once approved, it ships to you.

Frequently asked questions

Is Assurance Wireless really free?

Yes. For qualifying Lifeline customers there is no cost for the phone or the monthly plan.

How long does approval take?

Most applications are reviewed quickly once eligibility documentation is provided.

Can I keep my current number?

In most cases yes — you can request to transfer (port) your existing number during enrollment.
