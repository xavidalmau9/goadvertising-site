How to Get a Free Phone in California (Assurance Wireless / California LifeLine 2026 Guide)

Updated June 2026 · GO Advertising — authorized Assurance Wireless partner

If you live in California and qualify for California LifeLine, you can get a free smartphone and free monthly service through Assurance Wireless — at no cost. GO Advertising (BGOA) is an authorized Assurance Wireless partner that helps eligible Californians enroll. Here's exactly how it works.

What you get

The California LifeLine free plan, on the T-Mobile network, includes:

  • A free basic smartphone (or bring your own compatible phone)
  • 12GB of high-speed data every month
  • Unlimited talk and unlimited text
  • $0 monthly cost

Keep your service active by using it at least once every 30 days.

Who qualifies in California?

You're eligible if you're a California resident in any one of these programs:

  • Medicaid / Medi-Cal
  • SNAP / CalFresh
  • Supplemental Security Income (SSI)
  • Veterans Pension Benefit or Survivors Pension
  • Federal Public Housing Assistance / Section 8

You may also qualify based on your household income. You'll need proof of program participation or proof of income. The benefit is limited to one per household.

How to apply (step by step)

  1. Confirm you're in California and qualify — check the program or income list above.
  2. Gather proof — your Medi-Cal/CalFresh card, a benefit letter, or income documentation.
  3. Apply on the official partner page — assurancewireless.com/partner/BGOA.
  4. Get your phone — approved customers can receive a free phone the same day, with final approval from the California LifeLine Administrator.
