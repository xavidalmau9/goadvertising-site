With recent changes in federal connectivity programs, many Americans are asking: "Is free phone service still available?" The answer is a resounding yes. While some programs have shifted, the Lifeline Assistance Program remains a permanent federal benefit designed to keep you connected to what matters most.

What the 2026 Lifeline benefit includes

Through our partnership with Assurance Wireless, qualifying customers receive a robust monthly plan on the T-Mobile network at $0 cost:

  • 10GB of high-speed data for browsing, jobs, and news.
  • Unlimited monthly minutes to stay in touch with family and doctors.
  • Unlimited text messaging nationwide.

How to qualify

You likely qualify if you or someone in your household participates in programs like SNAP (food stamps), Medicaid, SSI, or Federal Public Housing Assistance — or based on your household income.
