Free Phone Service for $0: How to Get Free Talk, Text & Data

January 3, 2026 · GO Advertising — authorized Assurance Wireless partner

In today's world, a reliable smartphone isn't a luxury — it's a lifeline. Whether you're searching for a job, staying in touch with your doctor, or making sure your kids can reach you, being disconnected isn't an option.

That's why GO Advertising partnered with Assurance Wireless to help eligible households access the federal Lifeline program. If you qualify, you could receive high-quality wireless service and a smartphone at absolutely no cost.

What do you get with the Lifeline plan?

This isn't a stripped-down plan. Through Assurance Wireless, eligible customers get a robust monthly package on the T-Mobile network:

  • FREE monthly data: 10GB of high-speed data each month (amounts vary by state).
  • FREE unlimited talk — no counting minutes.
  • FREE unlimited text.
  • FREE smartphone: qualifying customers may receive a free Android™ smartphone.
  • Keep your phone: already love your phone? Bring your own (BYOP) and just get the free SIM and service.

Do I qualify?

Eligibility is based on government-assistance participation or household income. You may qualify if you or someone in your household participates in:

  • SNAP (food stamps)
  • Medicaid
  • SSI (Supplemental Security Income)
  • Federal Public Housing Assistance (Section 8)
  • Veterans & Survivors Pension Benefit

You can also qualify if your household income is at or below 135% of the Federal Poverty Guidelines.

Why Assurance Wireless?

It runs on the T-Mobile network (covering 99% of Americans), with no activation fees, no monthly bills, and no credit checks — plus free tools to help block robocalls and scams.
