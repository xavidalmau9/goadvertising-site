What is the California LifeLine offer?

California's program is unique. Because of state-specific funding, Assurance Wireless provides an "enhanced offer" unavailable in most of the country. When you qualify in California, you receive:

  • A FREE Android™ smartphone (models vary by inventory — brands like Samsung and Motorola).

Do I qualify for a California free phone?

You likely qualify if you or someone in your household participates in any of these programs:

  • Medi-Cal / Medicaid
  • CalFresh (SNAP / food stamps)
  • Supplemental Security Income (SSI)
  • Section 8 / Federal Public Housing Assistance
  • Veterans Pension or Survivors Benefit

Income-based eligibility: even if you aren't in a government program, you may still qualify if your household income is at or below the California state guidelines.
