Yes — if you qualify for the federal Lifeline program, you can get a free smartphone and free monthly service through Assurance Wireless at no cost. Here's exactly what you get, who qualifies, and how to apply.

What you get

Assurance Wireless is a federal Lifeline-supported program. Qualifying customers receive a free smartphone, free monthly high-speed data, unlimited texts, and monthly minutes — at $0 cost, funded by the government Lifeline benefit.

Eligibility

Who qualifies?

You likely qualify if you're in any one of these programs — or meet the income guideline.

  • Medicaid
  • SNAP (food stamps)
  • Supplemental Security Income (SSI)
  • Federal Public Housing Assistance
  • Veterans Pension & Survivors Benefit
  • Income ≤ 135% of Federal Poverty Guidelines

The Lifeline benefit is limited to one per household.

FAQ

Free government phone — your questions

Is the Assurance Wireless phone really free?
Yes. For customers who qualify for the federal Lifeline program, the Assurance Wireless smartphone and the monthly plan are free — no cost for the device or the monthly service.
Who qualifies for a free government phone?
You qualify if you participate in Medicaid, SNAP, SSI, Federal Public Housing Assistance, or Veterans Pension — or if your household income is at or below 135% of the Federal Poverty Guidelines. One benefit per household.
How do I apply for an Assurance Wireless free phone?
Confirm eligibility, gather your proof, then apply online at the official partner page: assurancewireless.com/partner/BGOA. Once approved, your phone ships to you.
Can I keep my current phone number?
In most cases yes — you can request to transfer (port) your existing number during enrollment.
Who is GO Advertising (BGOA)?
GO Advertising is an authorized marketing partner that connects eligible individuals to the Assurance Wireless Lifeline program. We don't set the benefits — we make applying simple.
