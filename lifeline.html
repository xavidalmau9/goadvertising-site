If you live in California and qualify, you can get a free smartphone and free monthly service through Assurance Wireless and California LifeLine — at no cost. Here's what you get, who qualifies, and how to apply.

What you get

The California LifeLine free plan on the T-Mobile network includes:

  • Free basic smartphone (or bring your own)
  • 12GB high-speed data every month
  • Unlimited text
  • Unlimited talk
  • $0 monthly cost
  • Same-day phone on Assurance approval

Just use your service at least once every 30 days to keep it active.

Eligibility

Who qualifies in California?

You likely qualify if you're a California resident in any one of these programs — or you meet the income guideline.

  • Medicaid / Medi-Cal
  • SNAP / CalFresh
  • Supplemental Security Income (SSI)
  • Veterans Pension or Survivors Pension
  • Federal Public Housing Assistance / Section 8
  • Qualifying household income

You'll need proof of program participation or proof of income. One benefit per household.

FAQ

Free California phone — your questions

Is the Assurance Wireless phone free in California?
Yes. Eligible California residents approved for California LifeLine get a free basic smartphone and free monthly service — 12GB high-speed data, unlimited talk and text — at $0. Available to eligible California customers only.
Who qualifies?
Participants in Medi-Cal, CalFresh, SSI, Veterans Pension/Survivors Pension, or Federal Public Housing/Section 8 — or those who qualify by household income. Proof required. One per household.
How do I apply?
Confirm eligibility, gather your proof, then apply at assurancewireless.com/partner/BGOA. Approved customers can get a phone the same day, with final approval from the California LifeLine Administrator.
How much data do I get?
12GB of high-speed data each month, plus unlimited talk and text on the T-Mobile network.
Is this available outside California?
This free-phone offer is for eligible California residents only. Lifeline plans in other states differ and may not include a free phone.
