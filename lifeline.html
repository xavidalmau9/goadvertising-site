If you qualify for Lifeline, you can get free phone service through Assurance Wireless — free monthly data, unlimited talk, and unlimited text at $0 a month. It's a federal Lifeline-supported service available nationwide. Here's what you get, who qualifies, and how to apply.

What you get

Free Assurance Wireless phone service on the T-Mobile network includes:

  • Free monthly high-speed data
  • Unlimited text
  • Unlimited talk
  • $0 monthly cost
  • Keep your current number
  • Free smartphone for eligible California customers

Data allotment varies by state (about 10GB in most areas, 12GB in California). In states other than California you can bring your own compatible phone. Use your service at least once every 30 days to keep it active.

Eligibility

Who qualifies?

You likely qualify if you're in any one of these programs — or you meet the income guideline.

  • Medicaid (Medi-Cal in CA)
  • SNAP (CalFresh in CA)
  • Supplemental Security Income (SSI)
  • Veterans Pension or Survivors Pension
  • Federal Public Housing Assistance / Section 8
  • Income ≤ 135% of Federal Poverty Guidelines

You'll need proof of program participation or proof of income. One benefit per household.

FAQ

Free phone service — your questions

What is Assurance Wireless free phone service?
A federal Lifeline-supported service. Eligible customers get free monthly high-speed data, unlimited text, and unlimited talk on the T-Mobile network — at $0 a month. Available nationwide.
Is a free phone included?
The free service (data, talk & text) is the core benefit and is available nationwide. A free smartphone is available to eligible customers in California; in other states you can bring your own compatible phone.
Which states is it available in?
Nationwide — every U.S. state except Hawaii, Alaska, and Puerto Rico.
Who qualifies?
Participants in Medicaid, SNAP, SSI, Federal Public Housing/Section 8, or Veterans Pension/Survivors Pension — or those who qualify by household income (≤135% of Federal Poverty Guidelines). Proof required. One per household.
How do I apply?
Confirm eligibility, gather your proof, then apply at assurancewireless.com/partner/BGOA.
