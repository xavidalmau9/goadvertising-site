Eligibility

Who qualifies?

You likely qualify if you're in any one of these programs — or you meet the income guideline.

  • Medicaid (Medi-Cal in CA)
  • SNAP (CalFresh in CA)
  • Supplemental Security Income (SSI)
  • Veterans Pension or Survivors Pension
  • Federal Public Housing Assistance / Section 8
  • Income ≤ 135% of Federal Poverty Guidelines

You'll need proof of program participation or proof of income. One benefit per household.
